Classes

We offer a variety of classes to help you train your furry friend!

Introduction to Dog Training

Saturday, October 5, 12:00 PM - 1:00 PM

All of our students are required to attend a one-time, introduction orientation prior to enrolling in any of our classes.
For all upcoming classes, the Introduction to Dog Training orientation will be held Saturday, October 5, 12:00-1:00 p.m. Cost is included with any class registration.
Prerequisites: None
$95 Register

Puppy Training

Sundays 2:00 PM

Puppy class is an open-ended class in which students are free to begin as soon as their puppy has had their second round of vaccinations. As you begin bonding with your new puppy, we will provide instruction on foundational training cues, health and nutrition, and basic puppy stages like house-breaking and crate training. Cost is $95.
Prerequisites: Introduction to Dog Training
$95 Register

Basic Manners

Thursdays at 11:00 AM or Sundays at 3:00 PM

An essential 4-week class that teaches good manners to dogs and responsible dog ownership to their owners. Manners training is more than the dog learning cues; it is teaching you to communicate with your dog in a manner your dog can understand. Classes include instruction for both ends of the leash on sit, down, come when called, loose leash walking, and much more. Cost is $95.
Prerequisites: Introduction to Dog Training
$95 Register

Challenge Course

Thursdays at 12:00 PM or Sundays at 4:00 PM

This course that exercises all the skills learned in Basic Manners with obstacles, games, and new challenges each week. Cost is $95. For students currently enrolled in a class through A New Leash on Life, the cost is $80 for classes.
Prerequisites: Introduction to Dog Training
$80 Register

Advance Manners

Thursdays at 1:00 PM or Sundays at 4:00 PM

An advanced 4-week class that continues to teach good manners to dogs and responsible dog ownership to their owners by building on the foundations of Basic Manners. Advanced Manners training is more than the dog learning cues; it is teaching you to communicate with your dog in a manner your dog can understand. Classes include instruction for both ends of the leash on stand, heel, distance down, go to your place, and much more. Cost is $95.
Prerequisites: Basic Manners
$95 Register

Loose Leash Walking and Recall

Thursdays, starting October 10 at 12 PM

A focused 4-week class built around mastering loose leash walking and a reliable recall. Get instruction, practice, and hands-on advice to improve these valuable skills. Cost is $95. For students currently enrolled in a class through A New Leash on Life, the cost is $80.
Prerequisites: Basic Manners
$80 Register

Urban Agility

Wednesdays, starting October 2 at 6:15 PM

Using natural items found in the outdoors, dogs will interact by jumping, climbing, balancing, crawling under, and going around various obstacles in their everyday world. Focus will be on both indoor and outdoor* obstacles (*weather permitting). Urban agility helps dogs with confidence and overcoming fears and is a low impact but stimulating activity for any canine. Cost is $100.
Prerequisites: Basic Obedience
$100 Register

Therapy Dog

New classes start each month

The goal of this class is to prepare both ends of the leash, people as well as dogs, for participation in our Therapy Dog program. Our Certification class is three weeks long, followed by a facility visit evaluation. Registration for class begins with a phone evaluation for us to get to know you and your dog. Cost is $120.
Prerequisites: Introduction to Dog Training
$120 Register
